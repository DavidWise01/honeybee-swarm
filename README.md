> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **LIFE SCIENCE**.

THE HONEYBEE SWARM (BEE) — a brain made of bees: a homeless swarm of ~10,000 must pick one new nest from a dozen, and almost always picks the best — with no bee comparing sites and none in charge. Thomas Seeley's mechanism: scouts waggle-dance with vigor tuned to site quality, positive feedback (better sites recruit faster), negative feedback (dances decay), and cross-inhibition (head-butt STOP signals — the neuron's own tie-breaker), sealed by a QUORUM at the cavity that launches the swarm. The neural analogy was made rigorously (Seeley/Passino/Marshall) — same equations as decision-making neurons. The macroscopic peak of the thread (kin to quorum-sensing + ant stigmergy). LIGHT vellum/purple theme; LIVE dance-and-quorum interactive. Living researchers cited; von Frisch (d.1982) + Lindauer (d.2008) minted. 11 emergents.
